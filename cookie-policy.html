Cookie Policy

This page explains how cookies are used on this website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are commonly used to help websites function properly and to provide information about how visitors interact with a site.

How Cookies Are Used on This Site

This website uses cookies only for analytics purposes through Google Analytics. These cookies help me understand how visitors use the site so that I can improve its content and performance.

Google Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics cookies may collect information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Browser and device type
  • Approximate geographic location
  • Traffic sources (such as direct visits or referrals)

This information is aggregated and does not personally identify individual visitors.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or remove cookies if you prefer. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how analytics tools function.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
